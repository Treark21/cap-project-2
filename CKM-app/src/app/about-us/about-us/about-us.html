Cobalt Keep Miniatures

Our Hobby

We all started the hobby as alot of people do. We saw the miniatures and thought that they looked awesome and wanted to play the games. Over the years we found that we enjoyed the painting aspect more and more. Our friends and family came to each of us asking for advice or to just paint their minis, and the ones that we did paint for always kept coming back looking for more.

Now we decided to expand just from our small community and offer our services for more people. This is because we believe that everyone deserves to have their dream army look as good in person as it does in their imagination.






Our Mission

Our commitment to quality is our mission. Our founder believed that not only should we offer exceptional workmanship in our painting but it should also be affordable.

We also make sure that your miniatures are painted in a quick and effecient manner that does not sacrifice the quality of the work. We are dedicated to the work and it shows.
