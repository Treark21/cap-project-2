Assembling

Assembling prices from this table represent the cost for asembling one miniature and it includes: cutting the parts from the sprues, cleaning the excesses, removing the mould lines, filling the gaps, gluing the parts together and drilling gun barrels and exhausts.

Painting

Painting prices from this table represent the cost for painting one miniature and sealing it with a protective varnish coat.

Payment

For starting to work on your order we will require an advance installment for 50% of the comission cost.

We accept PayPal, and may offer other payment methods on request.

Changes to the project may incure additional fees that will be discussed at the time of change.

Finalizing your order

Only after the miniatures are finished and you are completely satisfied with the provided pictures of them, we will ask for the rest of the payment.

Once we receive confirmation of it we will ship the miniatures to you, and provide you with a tracking number.

Shipping

We bubble wrap the miniatures and put them in a box filled with bubble foil, so you can be certain your huge armies and delicate models will arrive in one piece.

Starting the process

To get started on having us make your dream army, please fill out the form located on your user page to get the process initiated!
